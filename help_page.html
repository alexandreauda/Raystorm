RULES AND TIPS
A NEW HOPE FOR YOU

Here they come! They came from the depths of the galaxy to invade our dear and beloved native planet.
More numerous, more powerful, better armed, the INVADERS are here!

In a last attempt to save our planet, the best scientists concentrated all their technological knowledge to build new generation of ships to try to push the invaders back to infinity and beyond.

So, if you want to live long and prosper, follow these instructions carefully, you must!

Move your ship with arrow
keys or with the joysticks of the
gamepad.

You can fire lasers with the space
bar or with the A (XBox) or X
(PlayStation) button of the gamepad.

You can also change the aspect
of your ship with LB/RB buttons
(XBox) or L1/R1 (PlayStation)
of the gamepad and put the game in
pause with 'p' or with
RT/LB button (XBox) or R1/L1
(PlayStation) of the gamepad.

The red stars you will try to catch, to obtain a
super attack for a few seconds and
the blue stars to obtain an invincible
shield for a few seconds too !

But be careful, the invaders get
faster and drop more bombs
as you complete each level !

And if you forget these
instructions later, view this page again, Luke!
