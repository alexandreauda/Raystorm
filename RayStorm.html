USERS

Move with arrow keys or with the joysticks of the gamepad, fire with the space bar or with the A (XBox) or X (PlayStation) button of the gamepad. You can also change the aspect of your ship with LB/RB buttons (XBox) or L1/R1 (PlayStation) of the gamepad and put the game in pause with 'p' or with RT/LB button (XBox) or R1/L1 (PlayStation) of the gamepad. Try to catch the red stars to obtain a super attack for a few seconds ! The invaders get faster and drop more bombs as you complete each level !
